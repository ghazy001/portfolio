About Hi, I'm Ghazi

Driven, Innovative, and Tech-Savvy

I’m a Computer Engineering student at ESPRIT, Tunisia, passionate about creating secure, scalable software solutions. With hands-on experience in cybersecurity, web development, and DevOps, I thrive on solving complex problems and pushing the boundaries of technology.

How I Got Here

My journey in technology began with a curiosity for how systems work, leading me to pursue a Scientific Baccalaureate at Avicenne Private High School. In 2021, I joined ESPRIT to study Computer Engineering, specializing in Software Engineering and Advanced Technologies. Through coursework in cybersecurity, software architecture, and DevOps, I built a strong technical foundation. Internships at BH Bank and Safran allowed me to apply my skills in real-world settings—conducting security audits and developing web applications. Each experience has fueled my passion for innovation and problem-solving.

Why Work With Me

I bring a blend of technical expertise and a proactive mindset to every project. My experience spans full-stack development (MERN, Symfony), cybersecurity compliance, and DevOps practices, making me versatile and dependable. Whether it’s optimizing a database at Safran or building an e-learning platform with face recognition, I focus on delivering practical, high-quality results.
